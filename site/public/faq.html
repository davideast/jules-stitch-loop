help Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about jules.top rankings, shipping, and data.

General

What is jules.top? expand_more
jules.top is the definitive leaderboard for developers using the Jules agent. We track commits, PRs, and "ships" to visualize who is building with maximum velocity.
How often is the data updated? expand_more
The leaderboard updates in real-time as Jules processes events. Feed data appears instantly, while aggregate stats and ranks are recalculated every few minutes.

Scoring & Ranking

How is the "Score" calculated? expand_more
Scores are a weighted composite of:
  • Commits (10 pts)
  • Pull Requests (50 pts)
  • Merges (100 pts)
  • Streak Bonuses (Multiplier)
How do I earn badges? expand_more
Badges are awarded automatically based on milestones (e.g., "100 Ships"), specific behaviors (e.g., "Night Owl"), or streak maintenance. Check the Achievements page for a full list.

Technical

Can I use the API? expand_more
Yes! We have a public API for fetching leaderboard data. Check the documentation link in the footer for endpoints and rate limits.
